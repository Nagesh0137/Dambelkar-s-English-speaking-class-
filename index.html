About Us

Welcome to Dambelkar's Spoken English Classes

Founded in 2010, Dambelkar's Spoken English Classes has been transforming lives in Ahmadnagar by empowering students with confident English communication skills. Our institute has helped over 5,000 students achieve fluency and confidence in English speaking.

Under the expert guidance of Prof. Rajesh Dambelkar, a renowned English language trainer with 15+ years of experience, we provide a unique learning environment that focuses on practical communication rather than just textbook knowledge.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our courses and admission process

Our courses vary in duration: Beginner's English (3 months), Intermediate (4 months), Advanced (5 months). Special workshops like Interview Preparation (2 weeks) and Public Speaking (1 month) have shorter durations. We also offer flexible timing options for working professionals.

We offer multiple batches throughout the day to accommodate different schedules: Morning (7-9 AM), Afternoon (12-2 PM), Evening (5-7 PM) and Weekend batches (10 AM - 1 PM). New batches start every month and you can choose a timing that suits you best.

Yes, we provide comprehensive study materials including textbooks, workbooks, audio CDs and access to our online learning portal. All materials are included in the course fee. We also provide additional resources for practice at home.

We offer a 10% discount for early registration (at least 15 days before batch starts), 15% discount for siblings enrolling together, and 20% discount for group registrations (3+ students). We also have special scholarships for meritorious students from economically weaker sections.

We provide recorded lectures for missed classes. You can also attend the same session with another batch (subject to availability) or schedule a one-on-one doubt clearing session with the faculty at no extra cost. Regular attendance is encouraged for best results.
